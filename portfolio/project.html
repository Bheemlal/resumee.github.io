TIC TAC TOE

Tic-tac-toe is a two-player game where players take turns placing marks on a 3x3 grid of cells. The goal is to be the first player to get three marks in a row, either vertically, horizontally, or diagonally. If the board is full but neither player has three in a row, the game is a tie.

The tic-tac-toe game is for two players. One player plays X and the other plays O. The players take turns placing their marks on a grid of three-by-three cells. If a given player gets three marks in a row horizontally, vertically, or diagonally, then that player wins the game.
